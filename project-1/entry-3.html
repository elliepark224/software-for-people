Collection Item 3

Diary

The third entry in my collection is my diary. I used to write diary entries really consistently when I was younger. Like pre-K, kindergarten, early elementary school. I have kept all of them, and they are really funny to look back on. It is interesting to see what I considered noteworthy back then. Most of my entries looked something like this:

  • Today I woke up.
  • I played with my friends Lily and Abby.
  • I went to my swim lesson.
  • I am writing this right now. I will sleep after this.
As I have grown older and busier, I have stopped writing diary entries. But the past couple years, I have felt scared that time has been passing by really fast, and that I barely remember my thoughts from the previous day, much less the previous weeks or months or years. I write sometimes now, but still not often enough to say I write consistently at all. But funnily enough, I bring this diary with me, back and forth between home and here. So that is another thing I carry with me.
