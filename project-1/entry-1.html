Collection Item 1

Polaroid of my dog

The first entry in my collection is a Polaroid photo of my dog that my Mom took. I had asked her to take a Polaroid photo of me holding my dog in my bedroom before I left for college and before my parents moved out of my childhood room. After I put my dog back down on the floor, my mom spontaneously snapped a shot of my dog looking up at her. I like this photo because it captures the exact angle that I usually see my dog at. I brought this photo with me to college and put it on my desk. Something to remember my dog and my life at home by.
